## Notes

- "모래 거울" — generated as _sand mirror_ (모래 = sand, 거울 = mirror). The
  prompt had 모레 (tomorrow), but the AI's reading creates an hourglass image:
  sand + glass. Preserved as-is.
- "말을 삼아놓니" — an AI-generated addition not in the original prompt. "Are
  you holding back the words?" / "Are you swallowing your words?" Adds a new
  tension to the chorus.
- "숨소리만 퍼지는 밤" — AI changed 방 (room) to 밤 (night). Preserved — a night
  of only breathing is more evocative.
- "너를 향해 손을 뻗어" — maps directly to Jiwoo's choreography: the extended
  arm reaching toward someone, originating from Isabelle's ballet extension.
- "You are the light through the broken glass" — the English refrain in Verse 2
  echoes Caspar finding VESPER's music in a dark corridor, light through
  fractured things.
- "깊이, 더 깊이, 가라앉는 목소리" — the Bridge describes Yuna's low register
  dropping "like a stone into water" (Ch11).
