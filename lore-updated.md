# SHIN-RYU: Master Lore Document

> Canonical state as of Chapter 12. All details below are established fact in
> the story. Chapters 1–12 cover roughly 4 weeks (early April to early May).

---

## 1. THE WORLD

**Setting:** Tokyo, present day. Spring advancing into early summer. The story
moves between Sakuragaoka High School (a public high school in Shibuya Ward),
the protagonists' apartments (Hiroo and Minami-Aoyama), and various Tokyo
neighborhoods.

**Tone:** Literary YA. Slow-burn romance with precision prose. Sensory density
(food, photography, skincare, darkroom chemistry). Emotional restraint
punctuated by moments of overwhelming honesty. Korean used for private/intimate
dialogue (always with inline translation). The writing alternates between
Isabelle's and Caspar's POV, sometimes within the same chapter.

**Structural pattern:** Each chapter has 4–6 sections separated by `---`.
Sections alternate between the two protagonists and their respective social
orbits. Major beats land in section endings. Time markers at section openings
(day, time, location).

## 2. THE PARENTS' PLOT

The families arranged for Caspar and Isabelle to attend the same public high
school in Tokyo, hoping proximity would produce a natural relationship
(corporate marriage merger). Both teens are aware of the manipulation. They have
discussed it explicitly (Ch8, Minami-Aoyama sidewalk) and have reclaimed it as
their own choice.

- **Caspar's view:** "It makes me angry that they think they can arrange this.
  But I would have found you anyway."
- **Isabelle's view:** "That's probably why they want us together." (Spoken
  aloud, acknowledged.)
- **Current status:** The parents' plan has succeeded. The relationship is real,
  but the characters own it now.

### House van Rijn / Shin (Isabelle's family)

- **Father:** Willem van Rijn. Dutch old-money banking dynasty (van Rijn Private
  Banking & Global Real Estate). Calls her _schatje_. Sends monthly allowances.
  Communicates sparingly but warmly.
- **Mother:** Shin Hee-yeon. CEO of Shin Entertainment, Asia's premier K-pop
  agency. Loves her daughter "fiercely, strategically, and from behind a desk."
  Sends gift baskets via assistant with printed signature. Personally selected
  every VESPER member.
- **Uncle Bastiaan van Rijn** (European Media Conglomerate) — NOT YET APPEARED.
- **Uncle Shin Dong-il** (Shin Telecom & Tech) — NOT YET APPEARED.

### House Waldstein / Ryu (Caspar's family)

- **Mother:** Helena Waldstein. CEO of Waldstein Botanicals & Aesthetics, the #1
  ultra-luxury skincare brand in Asia. Design philosophy: things should look
  "inevitable." Sends shipped goods (jam from Vienna, pretzels from Munich,
  custom toaster from Darmstadt, quarterly shampoo cases). Expresses love
  through materials.
- **Father:** Ryu Ji-seok. Chairman of Ryu BioPharma & Chemical. Communicates in
  sentence fragments and single 👍 emoji. "An extravagant display of emotion."
- **Uncle Klaus Waldstein** — sent Caspar to the hotel (not named in dialogue
  but implied).
- **Aunt Ryu Myung-hee** (Ryu Retail) — NOT YET APPEARED.

### The Aunties (Major Characters)

- **Auntie Choi (Choi Eunji):** Isabelle's housekeeper. Late 50s, center-parted
  bob, navy linen apron. Strict, precise, protective. Cooks Korean breakfasts
  (doenjang-jjigae, galbi, samgyeopsal). Inspects cold chain documentation at
  Kinokuniya. Starches blouses twice. Coordinates nightly with Auntie Park.
  Calls Caspar's housekeeper "that woman" (affectionate rivalry). Has a kitchen
  torch and opinions about meringue browning.
- **Auntie Park:** Caspar's housekeeper. Tall, broad-shouldered, immaculate
  perm, cat-print apron. Louder than Auntie Choi. Better cook (by Caspar's
  admission; Auntie Choi suspects). Makes hand-pulled noodles, homemade
  gochujang, dakgalbi, jjajangmyeon (transcendent). Distributes praise "the way
  a central bank distributes currency." Cried when Caspar first smiled
  (post-kiss). Coordinates nightly with Auntie Choi.
- **Their relationship:** Nightly phone calls sharing household intelligence,
  emotional assessments of the teens, and culinary one-upmanship. They are a
  parallel diplomatic channel.

## 3. ISABELLE / SHIN JI-WON (15)

### Appearance

5'10", heavy wavy caramel-blonde hair, hazel-green upturned eyes, "glass skin"
(mul-gwang), flawless posture from ballet + idol training. Tallest person in
most rooms.

### Backstory

- Grew up in Wassenaar, Netherlands. Attended Institut Le Parcours (Swiss
  boarding school) from age 11, where she met Caspar.
- **The terrace incident (age 14):** Isabelle tried to kiss Caspar on a stone
  terrace in Switzerland. He turned away at the last moment. She sent three
  messages: _"I'm not angry at you. I need you to know that. I'm just not ready
  to talk about it yet."_ He never replied. A year of silence followed.
- **Gap year in Seoul:** Her mother placed her at Shin Entertainment as a
  training participant (not a trainee; never going to debut). She trained with
  VESPER for 10 months: 12–14 hour days, dance at 6 AM, vocal lessons,
  performance evaluations. She served as a "test body" for choreographer Park
  Jisoo. Her ballet technique directly influenced Jiwoo's signature extension.
- Now in Tokyo, attending Sakuragaoka High, maintaining the secret of her idol
  training.

### Skills & Habits

- **Baking:** Professionally trained (patisserie intensive near the Bastille,
  instructor was a Meilleur Ouvrier de France). Can produce madeleines, tarte au
  citron, éclairs, financiers, mille-feuille at Michelin-level quality. Her
  kitchen: La Cornue range, Carrara marble counters, KitchenAid Professional,
  copper bowls. She bakes to process emotions.
- **Dance:** 5 years ballet in Amsterdam + 10 months K-pop training. Can perform
  all VESPER choreography from memory. Her body responds involuntarily to dance
  (mirror response, micro-contractions).
- **Language filter:** Pretends her Japanese is weaker than it is (deliberate
  pauses, slight hesitation). Uses it to weed out fake friends and control
  perception. Also fluent in Dutch, Korean, English, French, German (academic
  competence).
- **Skincare:** 8-step Waldstein Botanicals clinical regimen (unreleased
  products from her mother's lab). SPF 50 PA++++.
- **VESPER group chat:** Active daily. The members are her family. They know
  about Caspar (told in Ch12).

### Apartment

Minami-Aoyama, 9th floor. Floor-to-ceiling windows. Arita-yaki ceramics. German
toaster (custom, no brand). La Cornue range. Designed by Helena's Tokyo office.
Doorman who calls her "Shin-sama." Hayashi-san (tutor/guardian, mid-30s,
rectangular glasses, drives a Toyota Crown).

### What She's Hiding

Her idol training is known to: Caspar (Ch10), the Aunties, her parents, VESPER
members. NOT known to: any of her school friends (Yuki, Sora, Hina suspect;
Kenji, Ren, Daiki do not).

### Relationship Status

**Dating Caspar** as of Ch9. First kiss: Wednesday after school, street corner
near school, after she asked "지금은?" (What about now?). Official via LINE: "내
여자친구 해줄래?" / "이미 그런 거 아니었어?" They now walk through the school
gate together, hold hands in public, and say "잘 자, 남자친구/여자친구" as their
nightly ritual.

## 4. CASPAR / RYU TAE-SUNG (15)

### Appearance

6'1", lean and muscular (broad shoulders, dense core from hotel labor). Dark
ash-blonde hair, pale blue almond-shaped eyes, striking aristocratic features.
"The energy of a cathedral." Stoic resting face that almost-smiles with
increasing frequency.

### Backstory

- Grew up in Bogenhausen, Munich. Same Swiss boarding school (Institut Le
  Parcours) as Isabelle.
- **The terrace incident:** Turned away from her kiss because "the wanting was
  so much bigger than I was." Regretted it for a year.
- **Gap year at Hotel Alpenhof:** Uncle Klaus sent him to work incognito as a
  bellboy/porter at a 5-star hotel in the Bavarian Alps. 12-hour shifts, 30-kilo
  suitcases up 4 flights of stairs, screamed at in 4 languages. Built physical
  strength, emotional resilience, and a deep hatred for oblivious wealth. Key
  mentors: Gerhard (retired ski racer, fitness), Maggie (Australian barista,
  coffee/patience), Frau Lindner (housekeeping, precision).
- Found VESPER during the hotel year (November, staff corridor, phone,
  algorithm-served dance practice video). Became a devoted secret fan.

### Skills & Habits

- **Photography:** Leica M6, shoots 35mm film. Tri-X 400 for B&W, Portra 400 for
  color. Has a darkroom (converted walk-in closet). Develops and prints his own
  work. Fan handle on VESPERspace: **@frame36** (reference to the last frame on
  a roll, always slightly overexposed). He shot Isabelle in color (Portra) at
  Daikanyama because "today was a color day." His photos of her are NOT for
  display — "These are mine."
- **Coffee:** Trained by Maggie. Prefers pour-over (V60). Knows extraction
  times, grind sizes, water temps. Orders pour-over reflexively, even at chain
  cafés.
- **Running:** Morning runs, 4K, route along the Furukawa River in Hiroo.
- **VESPER fandom:** Has memorized their entire discography, fan chants, and
  point choreography. Collects photocards (keeps them in a protective sleeve in
  his darkroom filing box). Owns a limited-edition holographic Yuna card from
  the debut showcase. Can perform "Glass Hour" chorus choreography with
  professional-level precision. Learned "Somebody Knows" opening sequence from
  Isabelle on a riverbank.
- **Language filter:** Same as Isabelle's. Japanese deliberately calibrated to
  suggest competence without fluency.

### Apartment

Hiroo, 5th floor. Renovated mid-century building. Pale oak floors, true white
walls (blank — waiting for his photographs). Minimal furniture: charcoal linen
sofa, photography books, paper-shade lamp. Concierge who calls him "Ryu-sama."
Hayashi-san (different from Isabelle's, also mid-30s, drives a Toyota Crown).

### What He's Hiding

His VESPER fandom is known to: Isabelle (Ch10, riverbank), VESPER members (Ch12,
via video). NOT known to: any school friends (Sora strongly suspects; Daiki
spotted him on VESPERspace app). His family wealth is partially exposed
(housekeeper slips, food quality) but not the full scale.

### Relationship Status

**Dating Isabelle** as of Ch9. He waited at the school gate for her (Ch9). She
linked arms with him in public (Ch9). He said "not yet" in the darkroom (Ch7)
and "now" on the street corner (Ch9). Official via LINE.

## 5. VESPER

**Agency:** Shin Entertainment (Isabelle's mother's company). **Members:** 4 —
Minji (leader), Yuna, Haeun, Jiwoo. **Debut single:** "Glass Hour." **Second
single:** "Somebody Knows" (dropped Ch11; 62 million streams in 3 days; trending
globally). **Fan community:** VESPERspace app. Lightsticks. Photocard trading.
Fan chants. **Training:** 3 years of scouting and training from hundreds of
candidates. Shin Hee-yeon values "endurance" over talent.

### Member Profiles

**Lee Min-ji (이민지) — Leader**

- Position: Right in formation. Sharp, precise movement.
- Personality: Maternal, responsible. Wakes at 5 AM, makes breakfast for
  everyone. Cries at sad movies, denies it, cries again. Corrects Isabelle's
  Korean grammar. The calm anchor. Lost an arm-wrestling match to Isabelle
  twice.
- Notable: Her body roll in "Glass Hour." The turn sequence in "Somebody Knows"
  (originated from Isabelle's ballet). Said "good form" about Caspar's choreo
  video.

**Seo Yuna (서유나) — Oldest**

- Position: Center-left. Main vocalist. Low voice, "lazy cat-smile."
- Personality: Explosive, excitable, chronically impatient. The group's chaos
  engine. Cannot wait for anything. Deeply loyal. Operates on instinct and
  warmth.
- Notable: Her bridge in "Somebody Knows" (took 3 takes; the engineer just
  nodded on the perfect one). Stole Isabelle's custard cream, left IOU with cat
  doodle. Recognized Isabelle at the fan event high-five (Ch11). Texted Caspar
  directly (Ch12): "울리면 도쿄 온다" (If you make her cry, I'm coming to
  Tokyo). Asked for his choreo video. Sending him a signed Type A photocard.
  Approved him.

**Haeun (하은)**

- Position: Back center. "Explosive power masked by a sweet smile."
- Personality: Warm, nurturing, physically affectionate. Braids Isabelle's hair
  while watching dramas. Took the chaotic group photo. Laugh-cried at Caspar's
  choreo video.
- Notable: Cried when eating Isabelle's tarte au citron in the practice room
  (tiredness + good food = tears).

**Jiwoo (지우) — Youngest (19 as of Ch12; birthday passed)**

- Role: Main dancer. Off-stage: quietest person Isabelle ever met. Soft Busan
  dialect. Stage presence that "burned so bright it was almost frightening."
- Notable: Her solo dance break in "Glass Hour" — the most-clipped moment of the
  debut. Borrowed Isabelle's ballet arm extension; practiced it for 45 minutes.
  Fans call her movement "ethereal." Her extension (originating from Isabelle)
  is one of the most-referenced images in K-pop fan art. Watched Caspar's choreo
  video three times without speaking ("the highest compliment"). Recognized
  Isabelle by TOUCH at the high-five event — felt the calluses on her palm.
- Wisdom: "Being scared is normal. Being scared means it's real."

### Isabelle's Role

Not a member. Trained with them for 10 months. Considers them family. They
consider her family. She receives their group chat, communicates daily. Her body
contributed to their choreography (Jiwoo's extension, Minji's turn in "Somebody
Knows"). She has told them everything about Caspar (Ch12). They have approved.

## 11. STYLE & VOICE GUIDE

- **Prose:** Dense, sensory, precise. Long sentences with embedded clauses.
  Metaphors drawn from photography, dance, food chemistry, and architecture.
- **Dialogue:** Minimal. Characters say less than they mean. Subtext carries the
  emotional weight. Korean dialogue is always translated inline in italics.
- **Humor:** Dry, character-driven. Kenji's chaos vs. Caspar's stoicism. Daiki's
  analytical absurdity. The Aunties' rivalry.
- **Pacing:** Slow. Time moves in days. Weeks pass across chapters. Major beats
  are earned through accumulation.
- **POV:** Third person limited, alternating between Isabelle and Caspar.
  Occasionally observes other characters from their perspective.
- **Emotional register:** Restrained. Characters rarely say what they feel
  directly. Physical details (pulse, breathing, jaw tension, hand pressure)
  carry emotion instead of stated feelings.
- **No quoted text in chapter bodies** — LINE messages are formatted as block
  quotes with translation. No other text appears in quotation marks for thoughts
  or messages.
- **Section breaks:** `---` between sections. Each section opens with a
  time/place marker.
