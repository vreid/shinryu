## Base Image Prompts

These are reference sheet prompts designed to establish each character's
consistent visual identity. Generate these first, then use them as
reference/LoRA/concept locks for scene images.

---

### 1. Takahashi Yuki

> Photorealistic portrait of a 15-year-old Japanese girl, Takahashi Yuki.
> Narrow, analytical face with high cheekbones and a sharp jawline. Dark
> straight hair cut in a precise shoulder-length bob, neatly brushed, no stray
> strands. Small, dark, observant eyes that assess rather than react. Thin lips
> that rarely smile — her rarest expression is a "ghost-smile": a softening of
> the mouth lasting one second, accompanied by a fractional crinkling of the
> eyes. Wears Japanese high school uniform: white blouse, navy sailor collar,
> pleated navy skirt. Carries a pencil case aligned precisely to the desk edge.
> Often seen holding a book. Build: slim, average height. Posture: impeccable,
> economical — no wasted movement. Expression default: calm, forensic, watchful.
> Photorealistic, natural lighting, plain background.

---

### 2. Yamamoto Sora

> Photorealistic portrait of a 15-year-old Japanese girl, Yamamoto Sora. Round,
> expressive face with wide eyes that narrow when excited or curious. Hair dyed
> a soft ash-brown with lighter highlights — trendy, fashion-forward, slightly
> tousled, with curtain bangs. Fuller lips that are almost always slightly
> parted mid-sentence or mid-reaction. Wears Japanese high school uniform: white
> blouse, navy sailor collar, pleated navy skirt — but accessorized with a
> VESPER silicone wristband she has never removed, and subtle earrings that
> violate dress code. Build: smaller frame, animated posture, hands and arms
> always in motion. Her ears turn a visible pink when she's excited or
> flustered. Expression default: open, eager, leaning forward. Photorealistic,
> natural lighting, plain background.

### 3. Ogawa Hina

> Photorealistic portrait of a 15-year-old Japanese girl, Ogawa Hina. Quiet,
> observant face with dark, steady eyes that see shapes rather than textures —
> the eyes of someone who notices everything and chooses what to reveal. Dark
> hair, simple, shoulder-length, no styling — practical and unadorned. Modest
> build, slight frame. She sits at the back of rooms and at the periphery of
> groups, watching rather than participating, until Ch15 when she turns to face
> inward and declares herself with the clearest voice she's ever used. Her rare
> smile is "slower this time, wider — the real one." Wears Japanese high school
> uniform: white blouse, navy sailor collar, pleated navy skirt. Shoes from a
> sale bin at Shimamura — slightly worn, practical. Expression default: calm,
> watchful, still — a face that holds everything behind a surface of neutrality.
> Photorealistic, natural lighting, plain background.

### 4. Watanabe Kenji

> Photorealistic portrait of a 15-year-old Japanese boy, Watanabe Kenji. Open,
> animated face dominated by a wide, uncomplicated grin that uses his entire

### 5. Ito Ren

> Photorealistic portrait of a 15-year-old Japanese boy, Ito Ren. Lean, athletic

### 8. Auntie Choi

> Photorealistic portrait of a Korean woman in her late 50s, Auntie Choi.

### 9. Helena Waldstein

## Scene-by-Scene Plan

### Chapter 1: Before the Gate

**1A. Isabelle at the mirror — Lines ~97-146**

- Isabelle in towel at bathroom mirror, morning light through floor-to-ceiling
  windows. School uniform laid out on the door with museum precision —
  knife-edge pleats, starched blouse, brown leather loafers. Her reflection:
  caramel-blonde hair, hazel-green eyes, examining her transformed post-Seoul
  appearance. The contrast between luxury world and school uniform she must now
  wear.
- Characters: Isabelle (ref)
- Notes: Solitary figure, interior, golden morning light, reflective surface
  composition.

**1B. The two nods — Lines ~716-776**

- School entrance hall, cherry blossoms drifting through open doors, settling on
  tile floor. Isabelle and Caspar across the crowded space, holding eye contact
  for five seconds across a threshold. Each gives a single calibrated nod —
  nothing more. Cherry petals in his dark ash-blonde hair. Her knuckles white on
  the shoe-locker edge.
- Characters: Isabelle (ref), Caspar (ref)
- Notes: Public space, institutional light, petals falling, extreme restraint in
  body language. The visual thesis of the story.

### Chapter 2: Parallel Lines

**2A. The hallway encounter — Lines ~719-840**

- Empty school corridor, flat fluorescent light, no shadows. Isabelle
  approaching with bucket and floor cloth, Caspar with broom. Honey tones in her
  hair catching the clinical light. Seven meters apart, then five, then three.
  Her hair pulled back with a pencil. His hand tightening on the broom handle.
  The moment he says "Isabelle" and she freezes.
- Characters: Isabelle (ref), Caspar (ref)
- Notes: Institutional, sterile lighting. The mundane made enormous. Tight
  framing on faces, shallow depth of field on the corridor.

**2B. Isabelle alone with the VESPER photo — Lines ~964-1000**

- Late-afternoon light throwing long parallelograms of gold across pale oak
  floors. Isabelle lying on her white linen bed in uniform, staring at the
  ceiling. On the wall above: a framed photograph of VESPER — four girls in
  black, laughing in a practice room, with a blurry fifth figure at the edge.
  That figure is her. Unopened gift basket with gold embossing on the side
  table. Phone in hand. The room turning gold around her.
- Characters: Isabelle (ref)
- Notes: Interior, golden hour light, loneliness composition. Photo within
  photo. The ghost figure at the edge.
