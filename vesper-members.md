# VESPER: Member Profiles

> VESPER (베스퍼) is a four-member K-pop girl group under Shin Entertainment.
> They debuted recently with the single "Glass Hour." A fifth member, Shin
> Ji-won (Isabelle), trained with the group but did not debut.

---

## Official Members

### Lee Min-ji (이민지) — Leader

- **Role:** Leader
- **Position in choreography:** Right
- **Movement style:** Sharp and precise
- **Personality:** The responsible, maternal figure of the group. Wakes at 5 AM
  to stretch, then makes breakfast for everyone. Cries during sad movies, denies
  crying, then cries again. Corrects Isabelle's Korean grammar with gentle
  persistence. The calm anchor who mediates between members—tells Yuna to calm
  down, tells the group to wait for Isabelle to share on her own terms.
- **Key details:**
  - Demanded a rematch after losing an arm-wrestling match to Isabelle in the
    Shin Entertainment break room. Lost again.
  - Described as having a body roll in "Glass Hour" that Sora is obsessed with.
  - "Minji on the right, body angled away from the camera but face turned toward
    it, her jawline catching the studio light."

---

### Seo Yuna (서유나)

- **Role:** Oldest member (unnie-line)
- **Position in choreography:** Center-left
- **Movement style:** Languid and controlled
- **Personality:** The most excitable and expressive member. Has a low voice and
  a "lazy cat-smile." Chronically unable to wait patiently. The group's chaos
  engine—explodes with enthusiasm in group chats, demands details, wants to know
  everything immediately.
- **Key details:**
  - Sora's bias ("Seo Yuna is my bias. She's unreal.").
  - Stole the last custard cream from Isabelle's secret snack drawer and
    replaced it with a handwritten IOU on a Post-it note: _"I owe you one (1)
    custard cream. Will repay in love and loyalty. – Y"_ with a small doodle of
    a cat.
  - Told Isabelle she's the best dancer in the group.
  - Eats instant ramyeon with training chopsticks and complains about her lower
    back hurting.
  - Gets hungry and demands international shipping for snacks.

---

### Haeun (하은)

- **Role:** Vocal/Performance
- **Position in choreography:** Back
- **Movement style:** Explosive power masked by a sweet smile
- **Personality:** Warm, nurturing, quietly affectionate. The member who braids
  Isabelle's hair while watching dramas on a laptop propped against the practice
  room mirror.
- **Key details:**
  - Took the chaotic group photo in the practice room that Isabelle keeps framed
    in her Tokyo apartment—said "Group photo, nobody pose, I want it chaotic."
  - Curious and supportive in the group chat—asks about Tokyo weather and cherry
    blossoms, tells Isabelle her baking is always delicious.
  - Laughed when Yuna exploded upon hearing Isabelle likes someone.

---

### Jiwoo (지우)

- **Role:** Main dancer
- **Age:** 18 (youngest member / maknae)
- **Dance style:** Stage presence that "burned so bright it was almost
  frightening." Off-stage, the quietest person Isabelle has ever met. Speaks in
  a soft Busan dialect.
- **Key details:**
  - Her solo dance break before the final chorus of "Glass Hour" is one of the
    most-clipped moments of the debut performance.
  - Borrowed a ballet arm extension technique from Isabelle—asked her directly:
    _"Your extension is longer than mine. Show me how you hold the last count."_
    Practiced it silently for 45 minutes and performed it on stage three days
    later. The adapted technique became a signature move, widely praised by fans
    as "ethereal."
  - Said to Isabelle: _"Unnie, your arms are better than mine. That's
    annoying."_
  - Sat beside Isabelle after a brutal rehearsal and said nothing, just leaned
    her head against Isabelle's shoulder.
  - Delivered the most quoted line in the group chat: _"Being scared is normal.
    Being scared means it's real."_
  - Profile shot in magazine covers: one hand raised near her face, fingers
    extended in a ballet-derived reach.

## Shadow Member

### Shin Ji-won / Isabelle (신지원)

- **Role:** Trainee (did not debut)
- **Relation to group:** Trained with VESPER for approximately one year in
  Seoul. Considered family by all four members, who call her _Ji-won-ah_ and
  _unnie._
- **Why she didn't debut:** Unknown / not yet revealed. Her mother, Shin
  Hee-yeon, is the CEO of Shin Entertainment and personally selected every
  VESPER member. Isabelle was sent to Tokyo instead.
- **Connection to the group's art:**
  - Her ballet training directly influenced Jiwoo's choreography (the signature
    arm extension).
  - She could perform the full debut choreography from memory.
  - She appears at the edge of the framed practice room photo in her Tokyo
    apartment—taller, lighter-haired, half-turned away, not realizing she was in
    the frame.
- **Current status:** Living in Tokyo under a different identity, attending
  Sakuragaoka High School. Maintains a private group chat with all four members.
  Her classmate Sora is an avid VESPER fan and has no idea she sits next to a
  former member every day.

## Known Relationships Between Members

- **Yuna & Isabelle:** Snack theft, custard cream IOUs, Yuna's conviction that
  Isabelle is the best dancer. Yuna is the most vocal about missing her.
- **Minji & Isabelle:** Grammar corrections, arm-wrestling rematches, quiet
  trust. Minji protects Isabelle's boundaries in the group chat.
- **Haeun & Isabelle:** Hair braiding, drama watching, the chaotic photo that
  captured all five of them. Physical affection without words.
- **Jiwoo & Isabelle:** The deepest artistic exchange—ballet technique traded
  for K-pop precision, practiced in silence, performed on stage.
  Shoulder-leaning. The line: _"Being scared means it's real."_
